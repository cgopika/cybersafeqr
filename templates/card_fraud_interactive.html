Key Warning Signs (Red Flags)

Hover over these cards to examine immediate warning indicators that signify an ongoing attack.

Asking for OTP

Banks NEVER ask for OTPs to check profiles or cancel payments.

Asking for CVV

CVV is a private local security code. Bank databases hold card profiles without CVVs.

Urgent Pressure

Scammers demand action "within minutes" to panic victims into ignoring rules.

Threats to Block

Threatening to block credit cards instantly is used to override caution.

Remote App Request

Asking to download AnyDesk or screen-shares lets them read codes off your screen.

Legal Framework

Understanding the statutory regulations penalizing digital financial fraud in India.

Information Technology Act, 2000

Section 66C – Identity Theft

Penalizes fraudulent use of unique identification details (like passwords, CVVs, or OTPs) belonging to another. Penalty includes imprisonment up to 3 years and a fine up to ₹1 Lakh.

Information Technology Act, 2000

Section 66D – Cheating by Personation

Covers cheating by impersonation using any computer resource (e.g. calling pretending to be a banker via VoIP). Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years and a fine up to ₹1 Lakh.

Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS)

Cheating and Fraud Provisions

BNS sections relating to cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property penalize scammers for extracting banking passwords to steal cash reserves.

Payment & Settlement Systems Act, 2007

Zero Customer Liability RBI Rule

Governs liability. If unauthorized card transactions occur due to bank negligence or third-party breaches reported within 3 days, the customer has **Zero Liability** for losses.

Safety Guidelines

Actionable safeguards to shield your cards and finances from theft.

Never Share OTPs

One-Time Passwords are 2FA authentication keys. Sharing OTPs equals sharing account permissions.

Hide your CVV

Never write down or reveal the 3-digit verification number printed on your card back.

Banks Never Ask Secrets

Your bank holds system admin rights. They will never query passwords, PINs, or credentials.

Verify Official Contacts

Only call numbers listed on bank statements or printed directly on physical card backs.

Enable SMS Transaction Alerts

Instant push/SMS notifications help detect unauthorized activity in real-time.

Use Secure Gateway Portals Only

Make payments only on certified web portals displaying security lock credentials.
